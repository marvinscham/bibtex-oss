About BibTeX OSS v0.3.3

BibTeX is great to work with but can be annoying to collect and structure, especially if you reference different types of media. BibTeX one-stop shop aims to make this a little easier by providing a simple interface to quickly look up DOIs, ISBNs and URLs to generate clean BibTeX references.

How does it work?

DOIs are looked up at doi.org, ISBNs are looked up at openlibrary.org and URLs will be scraped for their meta infos (JSON-LD, Open Graph, meta tags, ...).

Any output will be put through BibTeX-Tidy to create a nice, uniform output you can easily copy to your clipboard for later usage.
